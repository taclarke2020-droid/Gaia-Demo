What is Gaia?

Gaia coin is a decentralised crypto system that automatically rewards verified land owners for restoring and maintaining healthy ecosystems on their land - with the money for these rewards coming from companies and donors who want to make real, measurable impact on the health of the planet.

Gaia Coin rewards ecological care twice - payouts for verified land health today and a crypto supply designed to become scarcer as ecological recovery grows.
